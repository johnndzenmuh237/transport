Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

🚚 Shipping

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available at checkout for $12. Overnight delivery is available for $24 on orders placed before 2pm EST.
Yes! All orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping. This threshold applies to the order total after any discounts are applied.
Currently we ship to the US, Canada, UK, Australia, and most EU countries. International shipping rates and times vary by destination and are calculated at checkout. Duties and taxes may apply.
Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order from the My Account page. Allow up to 24 hours for tracking to activate.

↩️ Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. Sale items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned.
Log in to your account, go to My Orders, and select "Return Item" next to the item you'd like to return. You'll receive a prepaid return label by email within 24 hours.
Once we receive your return, we'll process the refund within 2 business days. Depending on your bank, the refund will appear on your statement within 3–10 business days.
We currently process exchanges as return + reorder. Return your item for a full refund, then place a new order for the size or color you need. This ensures the fastest turnaround.

👗 Products

💳 Payment

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely via Stripe.
